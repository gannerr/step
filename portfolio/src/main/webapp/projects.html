To get a little more serious, here are some projects I've worked on in the past:

  • Food Desert Paper

    • This is basically a cool research paper my friends and I wrote that investigates the rising problem of food waste and food insecurity (basically people don't have access to enough nutrition for healthy living). Using a bunch of math, and stalking datasets, we discovered that it was theoretically possible to repurpose food waste to feed the food-insecure population. A surprising fact: the food waste generated in Texas can actually be used to feed the State's entire food-insecure population! Just a rag-tag bunch of teenagers looking to create change...with math :D

  • Roomr.

    • My group's final project from Google CSSI 2019. Roomr is a social media platform for college students to find potential roommates. It has everything users need to connect with one another, from a friending system to a live-chat feature. This was an awesome project to build, especially given that everyone in my group basically had no web-development knowledge 3 weeks prior. We truly went big-brain on this one. Shoutout to Reeve, Zak, and Quinn :)
